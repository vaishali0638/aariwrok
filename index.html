Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aari work?

Aari work is a type of intricate hand embroidery done using a needle with a hook, known for its fine detailing using beads, threads, stones, and sequins.

Do you take custom blouse orders?

Yes! We specialize in custom bridal and festive blouse designs. You can send your measurements and reference images via WhatsApp or our order form.

How long does it take to complete an order?

It usually takes 5–10 days depending on the design complexity. Urgent orders are taken based on availability.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently, we deliver all over India. For international orders, please contact us directly on WhatsApp.
